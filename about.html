ABOUT SIRI LAKSHMI

Siri Lakshmi offers Transformation through Kundalini Yoga, Meditation, Reflexology & Metamorphic Technique

Indira Siri Lakshmi has been teaching Yoga and practicing Complementary Therapies for the last 11 years. She has made gradual transition from full-time career in Banking, the Visual Arts and Architecture to Complementary Therapies.  Using Reflexology, the Metamorphic Technique and Kundalini Yoga, Indira aims to brings about sense of equilibrium for the clients and students.

She teaches Kundalini Yoga for the Adult Education programme and Evening classes for a wide range of students.  The student are from a diverse background such as the Elderly, Adults with Autism and Learning Disability, unemployed, those with mental health problems and students from the mainstream population. She also teaches in schools to the children ages 11-15.   The Complementary Therapies, Kundalini Yoga and Meditation are tools to live a happier and healthier life.  She teaches 1-1 session and small groups.

Indira Siri Lakshmi is studying to become a Kundalini Yoga Therapist. The Therapy views the client as an energetic being. This method involves use of Kundalini Yoga teachings, such as the 10 bodies, acupressure points, Meditation, Yoga, movement and the Chakra system to recalibrate the body.

Her aim is to enable clients to create a natural balance in their daily life and empower them to transform and develop. Many obstacles in life are connected to emotions.
