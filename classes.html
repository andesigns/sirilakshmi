Kundalini Yoga

Kundalini Yoga has all the tools for humans to create prosperity in their life – material wealth, spiritual wealth, physical health. Each week we will practice Kriyas (sequence of postures) and meditations to embrace our unique qualities through postures and breathing techniques to abandon negativity around and within us. You will also be able to understand how colours have an effect on your sense of wealth, health and abundance.
